<h1>Assessment</h1>
<p><strong>ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK</strong>
<br>Assessment refers to the ongoing gathering of information on different
facets of a child from various sources, with the aim of providing qualitative
and quantitative feedback to support and guide the child's development.</p>

<p><strong>Important Note:</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Any student who is absent for a weighted assessment needs to produce a
Medical Certificate to cover him for that day. Marks will be pro-rated
accordingly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For Weighted School Preliminary Examination and Weighted End-of-Year Examination,
there is no make-up for Listening Comprehension and main written papers.
Students will not be eligible for academic awards if they are absent for
the main written papers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Weighted assessment to be conducted on the same day during respective
subject periods.</p>
</li>
</ol>
