<h3>Cadet Scouts</h3>
<h4>Overview</h4>
<p>The Cadet Scouts Movement aims to develop good citizenship among our pupils
through:&nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>observation, obedience and self-reliance</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>inculcation of loyalty, integrity and thoughtfulness towards others</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>teaching of services useful to the public and skills useful to themselves</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>promotion of physical and mental development.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Membership is open to pupils from Pri.3 and Pri.4. Activities include
drills, knotting, games, hiking, camping and first-aid, as well as working
on progress (gold, silver and bronze) and proficiency badges.&nbsp;(e.g.
sportsmanship, book reader, young naturalist etc.)</p>
