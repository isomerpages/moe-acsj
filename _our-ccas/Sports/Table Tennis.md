<h3>Table Tennis</h3>
<h4>Overview</h4>
<p>Table Tennis CCA has always been very popular in school. Its members are
dedicated and enthusiastic towards the game and have excellent discipline,
teamwork and sportsmanship.</p>
<p>Members hone and perfect their skills weekly so as to compete at the Zone
and National Primary Schools tournaments.</p>
