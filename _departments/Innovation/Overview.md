<h1>Overview</h1>
<p>Innovation is&nbsp;<em>something different</em>&nbsp;<em>that creates value</em>.
The vagueness of the word&nbsp;<em>something</em>&nbsp;is a reminder that
innovation is not the job of the few but the responsibility of the many. <em>Creating value</em>&nbsp;distinguishes
innovation from its precursors, such as creativity and invention.</p>
